About Me

Hello! I'm Ben Williams, a dedicated Sound Designer with a passion for bringing ideas to life. I specialize in C++, creating engaging and immersive experiences. My technical skillset extends to C# and LUA, giving me the flexibility to work on a variety of platforms and frameworks.

I'm also proficient in the use of Reaper and FL Stuidio, with hands-on experience in Unreal Engine 5 and Unity. I'm currently in the exciting phase of developing my first audio experience called Nebula, which aims to demonstrate my skills and creativity.

I'm a problem-solver at heart, always eager to tackle challenges. My versatility in coding languages and frameworks along with my familiarity with DAWs equips me well for any project.

Currently open to new opportunities, I'm excited to contribute my skills and enthusiasm to a like-minded team or project. Feel free to explore my skills and projects below!

Note: I made this website from scratch using the full MERN stack along with HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. It is hosted using a custom domain.

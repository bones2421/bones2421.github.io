About Me

Hello! I'm Ben Williams, a dedicated Sound Designer with a passion for bringing ideas to life through immersive audio experiences. Specializing in Unreal Engine, I create engaging and dynamic soundscapes that captivate audiences.

With proficiency in C#, C++, and LUA, I bring flexibility to work on various platforms and frameworks. I'm also skilled in Reaper, FL Studio, Wwise, and FMOD, and have hands-on experience in Unity.

Currently, I'm developing my first audio experience called Nebula, designed to showcase my creativity and technical prowess. Stay tuned for its launch!

As a natural problem-solver, I'm always eager to tackle new challenges. My versatility in coding languages and familiarity with DAWs make me well-equipped for any project.

I'm actively seeking new opportunities to contribute my skills and enthusiasm to a like-minded team or project. Explore my skills and projects below, and feel free to reach out!

Sound Design Skills:Foley, Mixing and Mastering, Adaptive Audio, Dialogue Editing and Processing, Problem-Solving, Collaboration, Attention to Detail, Sound Asset Management, Spatial Audio, and more
